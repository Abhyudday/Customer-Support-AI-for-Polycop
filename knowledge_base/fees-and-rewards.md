# Fees & Rewards

### **What are the fees for using the bot?**&#x20;

* **Gas Fees**: $0. You do not need to pay POL (MATIC) for gas; the bot handles this for you.
  * Only a transaction fee will be charged; no transfer fees will be applied.
  * Send **USDC.E (Only USDC.E, Only Polygon Network)** to the trading address on Polygon Network (No fee)&#x20;
  * Send **USDC or USDT** to the deposit address (Low Cross-chain bridge fee / Very Low Swap fee).
* **Transaction fee:** 0.5%. that's half the market rate, the lowest one on the market.

All the data is available on the blockchain explorer. You can enter your address on Polygonscan and download the CSV file of your transaction history to calculate it.

### **How does the referral program work?**&#x20;

* Share your link and earn! Rewards start at **25%** of the fees generated by your referrals and can go up to **45%** based on volume. Send `/referrals` to get your link.
