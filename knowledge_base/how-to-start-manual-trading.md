**You can find a market in two ways:**

<figure><img src="https://1959142348-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FdDRdNQwMVGJeqH9yLJQL%2Fuploads%2FP8nqsf1CaD5vT6Uq8Dal%2F01f0641523b7b7af32919491a38044f1.jpg?alt=media&#x26;token=d1ae0a2f-7182-48ac-8f99-8c5245d514b1" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. **Paste a Link:** Simply paste a Polymarket market link into the chat.
2. **Search by Text:**&#x20;
   * Type keywords to search for a market (e.g., "Bitcoin", "Trump").
   * The bot will show a list of matching markets for you to choose from.

**Then just Click "Buy":** Click the `Buy` button in the main menu, and the bot will ask you to enter a link.
